Information About our team

Our project, conducted under the guidance of Asst.professor Priya K V, focuses on establishing a sustainable Water cleaning initiative within our community. The aim is to integrate environmental education,public awareness, and developing skills to promote sustainability and healthy living

Undertaking this project under Asst.professor Priya's guidance not only enhances our academic understanding but also instills a sense of responsibility towards our environment. It's a testament to the integration of education with real-world applications, preparing us for a future where sustainable practices are essential for the well-being of our planet.
